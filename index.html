Research

Cybersecurity

I'm a total NUT for learning whatever I can when it comes to cybersecurity! Creative Writing

Writing has been a long-standing passion of mine since childhood. I'm a year into a massive worldbuilding project for a book series I'm writing, and the journey has led me to the wonderful opportunity of being able to combine all three of my interests into one. I couldn't ask for more!

My road started in the medical field and I discovered that while fulfilling, my passions lied elsewhere. Many life changes caused me to reorient myself and my priorities, so now I aim to add cybersecurity to my resume!
